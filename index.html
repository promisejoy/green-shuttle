About the Initiative

A Gift to the People of Abia State

The Abia Green Shuttle is a flagship transportation initiative launched by the Abia State Government to provide safe, and reliable public transportation for all residents of Abia State.

This initiative reflects our commitment to improving the quality of life for Abians, reducing transportation costs, and connecting our vibrant communities across the state. The service is currently completely free as a gift from the government to the people.
