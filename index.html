Executive Governor

Gov. Alex Chioma Otti, OFR

In December 2025, we launched Nigeria's first state-led electric public transportation initiative - the Abia Green Shuttle. This groundbreaking project represents our commitment to sustainable development, technological innovation, and improved quality of life for all Abians.

"The Green Shuttle is more than just transportation; it's a statement of our commitment to environmental sustainability, economic growth, and modern infrastructure. We're building an Abia State that works for everyone - cleaner, greener, and more connected than ever before."

With an initial deployment of 20 electric buses and plans to expand to 100, powered by solar charging infrastructure, the Green Shuttle connects our major cities while reducing carbon emissions and transportation costs. This initiative demonstrates how visionary leadership can transform public service delivery in Nigeria.

Governor Alex Chioma Otti, OFR

INITIATIVE OVERVIEW

Nigeria's First Electric Public Transport

Launched in December 2025, the Abia Green Shuttle represents a revolutionary approach to public transportation in Nigeria, combining environmental sustainability with modern technology.

Transforming Transportation in Abia

The Abia Green Shuttle is a pioneering public transportation initiative featuring electric-powered buses designed to reduce transportation costs and promote environmental sustainability across Abia State.

Dec 2025
Launch Date
400-450km
Bus Range
Coscharis
Manufacturing Partner

The service initially deployed 20 electric buses with plans to expand to 100, operating across Aba, Umuahia, and Ohafia. Each bus is equipped with charging ports, CCTV cameras for security, and is fully accessible for persons with disabilities.

MODERN FEATURES

Advanced Electric Bus Features

Our electric buses are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and amenities for a comfortable, secure, and sustainable travel experience.

Solar Powered

Charged by 10 dedicated solar charging stations with battery storage systems for continuous operation.

Air Conditioned

Fully air-conditioned cabins for comfortable travel regardless of external weather conditions.

CCTV Security

24/7 recording cameras ensure passenger safety and security throughout the journey.

Disability Access

Specially designed for easy access by persons with disabilities, including wheelchair ramps.
